## The BlueHats Manifesto

```
Civil servants should be free to use Free Software.

Civil servants should be free to contribute to Free Software.

Digitisation of public services should rely on Free Software.

Governments should be involved in the governance of strategic Free
Software projects and contribute to their sustainability.
```
